About us


At Kaloo, we bring the timeless tradition of Basque craftsmanship to life through our exquisite handmade furniture. Rooted deeply in the rich cultural heritage of the Basque Country, our workshop combines age-old techniques with a modern design sensibility, creating unique pieces that tell a story of passion, skill, and respect for natural materials.


Every piece of furniture we create is meticulously crafted by our artisans using sustainably sourced wood and traditional joinery methods passed down through generations. We believe that furniture is not just functional—it is an expression of identity, comfort, and artistry that enhances the spaces where life unfolds.


Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and environmental responsibility guides every step of our process. From selecting the finest raw materials to applying the finishing touches by hand, we ensure that each item meets the highest standards of durability and beauty.


Based in the heart of the Basque Country, we are proud to carry forward a legacy of craftsmanship that celebrates creativity, tradition, and innovation. Whether you are seeking a bespoke dining table, a classic chair, or a statement piece, Kaloo offers furniture designed to last a lifetime and be treasured for generations.
